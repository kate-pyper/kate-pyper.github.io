Biography

Kate Pyper is a teaching assistant at the University of Strathclyde. She is currently involved in teaching introductory classes in regression, statistical programming in R and spatial statistics as well as developing and running materials for an online MSc in Applied Statistics in Health Science. She is particularly interested in how learning technology can be utilised to enhance the student experience.

Kate is also the chair of the RSS Glasgow Local Group and is a founding organiser of the R-Ladies Glasgow Chapter.
